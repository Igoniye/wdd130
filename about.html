Founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson,White Water Rafting Adventures began with a single raft and a passion for sharing the thrill of river running. What started as weekend trips for friends has grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region, with over 25 years of experience navigating the wildest rivers safely.
